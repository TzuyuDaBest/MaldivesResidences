Affordable Property Investment in Penang

Maldives Residences is located in the vibrant area of Bayan Lepas, in Penang, offering a central living experience with easy access to various essential facilities. Situated along Jalan Sungai Tiram 6, this residential development is just under 2 km from Penang International Airport, ensuring convenience for frequent travelers. The development is well-connected to major routes like Jalan Tun Dr Awang, offering smooth access to both the city and other regions on the island.

Strategic Location

Living here means being close to various amenities, such as shopping centers like Queensbay Mall (9 km away) and Giant Hypermarket Bayan Lepas (4.4 km), as well as healthcare facilities like Pantai Hospital Penang and Pearl Maternity Hospital. The surrounding residential and commercial projects add to the area's vibrancy, making Maldives Residences a prime location for both work and leisure.
